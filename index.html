Skills

I enjoy design more than research, but I'm well versed in both. I think in order to be a good designer, you have to be able to understand and empathize with your user, so to me they go hand-in-hand. I love coming up with new ideas to a problem and sharing them, which is why I enjoy brainstorming, wireframing, prototyping, etc. I also have a basic understanding of a few coding languages and web dev, including HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, Python, and Bootstrap.
