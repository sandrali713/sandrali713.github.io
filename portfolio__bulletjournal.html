My Bullet Journal

Layout Design

Bullet Journaling is something I've been doing since Junior year of high school. It's a way for me to both stay organized and have a creative outlet in my day-to-day life. It's been increasing in popularity in recent years, with the growth of YouTube and Instagram. Basically, it's a combined planner and journal, but it comes as a blank journal so you draw in the templates yourself. This way, it's customized to your needs and preferences. I love that it gives me the freedom to design and draw however I'd like, but it's also functional and keeps me productive.
