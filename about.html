My Story

Hi there! Thanks for checking out my portfolio. It is a work in progress and I'm sure it'll keep changing as I change and grow, but I hope it offers you a glimpse of what Iâ€™ve accomplished and am working on.

I call myself an engineer because I love building things. I fell in love with engineering in high school, because it combined my desire to help people and solve problems with the background of math and science that I grew up with.

I call myself a designer because creativity is my freedom. I've played with all kinds of art since I was a kid, from spending hours trying to hollow out eggs so I could paint them on Easter, to designing a wooden roof for my treehouse, to using computer-aided design software to 3-D print components of a robot. Every bone in my body longs to create something new, to play with forms and spaces, and to experiment without limitation.

I call myself a dreamer because it's what I've always been. I have big goals for both myself and as a hope for the kind of society we could be. I believe we could live in a kinder, smarter, safer world, where we value the planet we live on just as we value each other, and I want to be a part of creating this world.

"If your dreams do not scare you, they are not big enough."
